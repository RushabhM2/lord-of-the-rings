The Journey has begun

Find Mt Doom and destroy the ring

Your moves

    The objective of the game is to find Mt Doom so that Frodo can destroy the One Ring.

    Navigate the map using directional keys, or "N", "S", "W", and "E" keys.

    To erase a move, hit backspace, to check if you made it, press ENTER or click "Did I defeat Sauron".

    On the way to Mt Doom you will have to dodge past Orcs, if your route passes Orcs, you will die.

    If you move off the map, or your path doesn't pass by anything, you lose.
